## Usage Guidelines

### Response Time Targets
- Download/Access Issues: < 4 hours
- Refund Requests: < 24 hours
- Pre-Purchase Questions: < 12 hours
- Feature Requests: < 48 hours
- Bug Reports: < 8 hours (acknowledgment)

### Tone Guidelines
- Friendly and conversational
- Empathetic to customer frustration
- Solution-oriented
- Professional but not corporate
- Always personalize with customer's name

### Customization Tips
1. Replace [bracketed] placeholders with actual information
2. Add specific details relevant to the situation
3. Adjust tone based on customer's email style
4. Include relevant links/resources
5. Always proofread before sending

### Follow-up
- If no response in 48 hours, send a gentle follow-up
- Close ticket after resolution confirmation
- Request feedback/review for positive interactions
