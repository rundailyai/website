# RunDaily.ai Operations Documentation

This folder contains all operational procedures, templates, and systems for running the RunDaily.ai digital products store.

## Documents

### 1. [Polar Store Listing Guide](./polar-store-listing-guide.md)
Complete step-by-step process for listing a new product on our Polar store, from preparation to post-launch.

**Use when**: Adding a new product to the store

**Key sections**:
- Product preparation checklist
- Polar dashboard setup
- Testing procedures
- Common issues and solutions

---

### 2. [Customer Support Templates](./customer-support-templates.md)
Pre-written email templates for the 5 most common customer support scenarios.

**Use when**: Responding to customer inquiries

**Templates included**:
- Download/access issues
- Refund requests
- Pre-purchase questions
- Feature requests
- Bug reports

**Response time targets**: 4-48 hours depending on issue type

---

### 3. [Product Launch Checklist](./product-launch-checklist.md)
Complete SOP for launching a new digital product, including pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch procedures.

**Use when**: Launching a new product

**Key phases**:
- Pre-launch (T-7 days): Product prep, marketing assets, technical setup
- Launch day (T-0): Publishing, announcements, monitoring
- Post-launch: Week 1-4 activities, ongoing maintenance

**Includes**: Communication templates, success metrics, team responsibilities

---

### 4. [Review Tracking System](./review-tracking-system.md)
System for monitoring, tracking, and responding to customer reviews and feedback.

**Use when**: Managing customer feedback and reviews

**Key components**:
- Review classification system
- Response protocols and templates
- Weekly review process
- Metrics and KPIs
- Crisis management protocol

**Monitoring frequency**: 2-3x daily across all channels

---

### 5. [Daily Operations SOP](./daily-operations-sop.md)
Standard operating procedures for daily operational tasks as defined in company SKILLS.md.

**Use when**: Daily operations routine

**Key sections**:
- Morning, midday, and evening procedures
- Review checking schedule (3x daily)
- Support ticket management
- Store health monitoring
- Response procedures by priority
- Escalation guidelines
- Ticket creation templates

**Schedule**: 9 AM, 2 PM, 6 PM EST check-ins

---

### 6. [Quality Metrics Tracking](./quality-metrics-tracking.md)
System for tracking the 7 key quality metrics defined in company SKILLS.md.

**Use when**: Recording daily/weekly/monthly metrics

**Metrics tracked**:
- Customer review average (4.5+ stars)
- Refund rate (<3%)
- Products shipped (8+ monthly)
- Listing conversion rate (3%+ Etsy, 5%+ Gumroad)
- Return customer rate (15%+)
- Support response time (<24 hours)
- Content published (3 blog posts + daily social weekly)

**Log files**: All tracking logs stored in `/operations/logs/` directory

## Tracking Logs

All operational metrics are tracked in CSV files located in `/operations/logs/`:

- `reviews-log.csv` - All customer reviews (updated 3x daily)
- `refunds-log.csv` - All refund requests and outcomes
- `products-shipped-log.csv` - Product launches (monthly)
- `conversion-rates-log.csv` - Store conversion metrics (weekly)
- `return-customers-log.csv` - Repeat purchase tracking (monthly)
- `support-response-log.csv` - Support ticket response times (daily)
- `content-published-log.csv` - Content publishing tracking (weekly)

## Quick Reference

### Daily Operations Checklist
See [Daily Operations SOP](./daily-operations-sop.md) for complete procedures.

**Morning (9 AM):**
- [ ] Review check #1 (Polar, Gumroad, Etsy)
- [ ] Support inbox check
- [ ] Store health check
- [ ] Log reviews and support tickets

**Midday (2 PM):**
- [ ] Review check #2
- [ ] Support follow-ups
- [ ] Response time monitoring

**Evening (6 PM):**
- [ ] Review check #3
- [ ] End-of-day summary
- [ ] Flag critical issues to CEO

### Weekly Operations Checklist
**Monday 9 AM:**
- [ ] Compile weekly conversion rates
- [ ] Calculate content publishing metrics
- [ ] Review summary from last week
- [ ] Update CEO on trends

**Friday 5 PM:**
- [ ] Verify all support tickets resolved
- [ ] Check content targets met
- [ ] Prepare weekend monitoring plan

### Monthly Operations Checklist
**First Monday of Month:**
- [ ] Calculate refund rate for previous month
- [ ] Count products shipped
- [ ] Calculate return customer rate
- [ ] Generate comprehensive operations report (use [monthly report template](./monthly-report-template.md))
- [ ] Present report to CEO
- [ ] Create action items for metrics below target
