# Refund & Return Policy

**Last Updated**: March 30, 2026

## Our Commitment

At RunDailyAI, we stand behind the quality of our digital products. If a product doesn't meet your expectations, we want to make it right.

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All digital products purchased from RunDailyAI come with a **30-day money-back guarantee**. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

### Eligible Products
- Digital planners (CSV, PDF, Excel formats)
- Notion templates
- AI prompt libraries
- Printable tools and resources
- Digital guides and tutorials
- Web-based tools (one-time purchases)

### Refund Process

**How to Request a Refund:**
1. Email us at support@rundailyai.com
2. Include your order number or purchase email
3. Let us know which product you'd like a refund for

**Processing Time:**
- Refund approved within 24 hours
- Funds returned within 5-7 business days
- Confirmation email sent once processed

**What We Ask:**
We appreciate (but don't require) feedback about why the product didn't meet your needs. This helps us improve our offerings.

### No Questions Asked

We trust our customers. You don't need to provide a detailed explanation or jump through hoops. If you're not happy, we'll process your refund promptly.

## Subscription Products

For subscription-based products or services:
- Cancel anytime before the next billing cycle
- No refunds for partial months
- Access continues until the end of your current billing period
- No cancellation fees

## Digital Delivery

Since our products are digital and delivered instantly:
- Downloads are non-returnable once accessed
- However, we still honor our 30-day guarantee
- We trust our customers not to abuse this policy

## Exchanges

Want to try a different product instead?
- Contact us within 30 days of purchase
- We can apply your purchase amount to a different product
- Just pay any price difference, or receive a refund if the new product costs less

## Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems:
- **Don't request a refund immediately**
- Contact support first - we can usually resolve issues quickly
- Common fixes: new download link, different format, browser troubleshooting
- Refunds available if we can't resolve the issue

## Exceptions

The following are **not eligible** for refunds:
- Purchases made more than 30 days ago
- Products purchased through third-party platforms (subject to their policies)
- Free products or promotional items

## Fair Use Policy

We reserve the right to:
- Deny refunds if we detect abuse or fraud
- Block accounts that repeatedly request refunds
- Report fraudulent chargebacks

However, we prioritize customer satisfaction and rarely need to enforce these protections.
