## 2. Pricing Recommendation

**Recommended Price: $24**

**Rationale:**
- Competitive positioning within the $19-29 range requested
- 5 comprehensive templates provide strong value
- Aligns with comparable products (Social Media Toolkit at $24)
- Lower than Notion Productivity Templates ($29) but more comprehensive than AI Content Calendar ($19)
- Price point is approachable for impulse purchases while reflecting quality
- Positions the product as a premium but accessible productivity tool

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Launch price: $24 (no discount needed—this IS the value)
- Optional: Limited-time launch bonus (future template or guide)
- Welcome email sequence discount (WELCOME20) brings it to $19.20
- Maintains profitability while being competitive

**Alternative Pricing Options:**
- Conservative: $19 (higher volume, lower margin)
- Premium: $27 (positions as flagship planning system)

**Recommendation: Stick with $24** — it's the sweet spot for this product.
