## Product Thumbnail Template (2000x2000)

### Purpose
High-resolution product thumbnail for digital store listings, marketplace platforms (Gumroad, Etsy, ProductHunt), app stores, and promotional materials.

### Design Specifications

**Canvas**: 2000x2000px (1:1 ratio)
**Format**: PNG-24 with transparency OR JPEG with solid background
**Color Space**: RGB
**Resolution**: 300 DPI (print-ready quality)

---

### Layout Design

**Background**:
- Primary Option: White (#FFFFFF) with subtle gradient to Light Gray (#F8FAFC)
- Alternative: Primary Blue gradient (135deg, #0066FF to #00D9FF) for premium products
- Pattern: Optional subtle dot grid (5% opacity) for tech aesthetic

**Border Treatment**:
- No hard border, but 100px safe margin from all edges
- Rounded corners: 40px radius for modern feel (if background has color)

**Main Content Area**: 1800x1800px (100px margin all sides)

---

### Primary Layout: Product Showcase

**Top Section** (200px height):
- Logo: RunDailyAI icon + wordmark
- Position: Left-aligned, 100px from top-left corner
- Size: 80px height for icon, wordmark 28px
- Color: Deep Navy on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds

**Center Section** (1200x1200px):
- Product Visual/Icon (large, centered):
  - Abstract representation of product/service
  - Geometric composition using brand colors
  - Size: 800-1000px depending on design
  - Style: Minimalist, modern, premium

**Geometric Elements**:
- Wave pattern from brand (curved parallel lines)
- Primary Blue and Bright Cyan gradient shapes
- Depth created through layering and subtle shadows
- Asymmetric composition for visual interest

**Product Title**:
- Font: Inter Extra Bold 800
- Size: 80px/88px
- Color: Deep Navy (on light bg) or White (on dark bg)
- Position: Center-bottom of center section
- Max width: 1600px
- Text align: Center

**Bottom Section** (400px height):
- Tagline or Key Benefit:
  - Font: Inter Medium 500
  - Size: 40px/48px
  - Color: Slate Gray or Bright Cyan (for contrast)
  - Position: Centered, 120px from bottom
- Category Badge:
  - Small pill shape: 160x48px
  - Background: Primary Blue or Bright Cyan
  - Text: White, Inter Semi-Bold 600, 18px
  - Examples: "Newsletter", "Digital Product", "AI Tool"
  - Position: Center-bottom, 60px from bottom

---

### Alternative Layout: Feature Highlight

**Background**: Primary Blue to Bright Cyan gradient (diagonal)

**White Content Card**: 1700x1700px, centered, rounded corners (32px)
- Card padding: 100px all sides

**Inside Card**:
- Product Icon: 400x400px, top-center
- Product Name: Inter Bold 700, 72px/80px, Deep Navy
- Subtitle: Inter Regular 400, 36px/44px, Slate Gray
- Key Features (3 items):
  - Icon + Text layout
  - Inter Semi-Bold 600, 32px
  - Vertical stack, 40px spacing
  - Icons: 64px, Primary Blue
- Logo: Bottom-center, 100px from bottom edge

---

### Design Elements

**Color Palette for Product Thumbnails**:
1. **Light & Fresh**: White bg + Primary Blue + Bright Cyan accents
2. **Bold & Premium**: Navy bg + Bright Cyan + White text
3. **Gradient Modern**: Blue-to-Cyan gradient + White card overlay

**Iconography**:
- Large central icon representing the product
- Style: Geometric, outline or semi-flat
- Colors: Use brand primary/secondary colors
- Size: 600-1000px for main icon
- Supporting icons: 80-120px

**Shadow & Depth**:
- Product icon shadow: 0 20px 60px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.15)
- Card shadow: 0 32px 80px rgba(0, 102, 255, 0.2)
- Layered elements: Use varied opacity and elevation

**Typography Hierarchy**:
1. Product Name: 72-80px, Extra Bold
2. Tagline: 36-40px, Medium
3. Features/Benefits: 28-32px, Semi-Bold
4. Category Badge: 18px, Semi-Bold

---

### Export Settings

**Primary Export**:
- Format: PNG-24 (with transparency)
- Dimensions: 2000x2000px
- Color Space: RGB
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- File naming: `RDAI_ProductThumbnail_[ProductName]_2000x2000.png`

**Alternative Sizes** (scaled versions for web use):
- 1024x1024px (marketplace preview)
- 512x512px (small preview)
- 256x256px (icon size)

---

### Usage Guidelines

**Do**:
- Keep design clean and uncluttered
- Use high contrast for readability at small sizes
- Feature one clear focal point (product icon/visual)
- Maintain brand colors and typography
- Include logo for brand recognition
- Test readability at 256px preview size

**Don't**:
- Overcrowd with too much text
- Use more than 3-4 colors
- Include low-resolution images
- Use thin fonts or small text (under 24px)
- Forget safe margins (100px minimum)

## Blog Post Header Template (1200x630)

### Purpose
Open Graph image for blog posts, social media shares, article previews. Optimized for Facebook, LinkedIn, Slack, and other platforms that generate link previews.

### Design Specifications

**Canvas**: 1200x630px (1.91:1 ratio)
**Format**: JPEG (smaller file size) or PNG-24
**Color Space**: RGB
**Resolution**: 72 DPI (web optimized)

---

### Layout Design

**Safe Zone**: 1120x550px (40px margin all sides)
- Some platforms crop edges, keep critical content in safe zone

---

### Primary Layout: Article Title Focus

**Background**:
- Option 1: White to Light Gray subtle gradient
- Option 2: Deep Navy with dot grid pattern (5% opacity)
- Option 3: Primary Blue gradient diagonal

**Left Content Section** (800x630px):

**Brand Header** (top):
- Logo: RunDailyAI icon (48px) + wordmark
- Position: 60px from top-left
- Below logo: "Blog" or "Insights" label
  - Inter Medium 500, 16px, Slate Gray
  - 12px below logo

**Article Title**:
- Font: Inter Bold 700
- Size: 52px/60px
- Color: Deep Navy (light bg) or White (dark bg)
- Position: Vertically centered in left section
- Max width: 700px
- Max lines: 3 (for readability)
- Padding: 60px from left edge

**Metadata** (bottom):
- Date and read time or category
- Font: Inter Medium 500, 18px
- Color: Slate Gray or Bright Cyan
- Position: 60px from bottom-left
- Format: "5 min read · AI Insights"

**Right Visual Section** (400x630px):
- Background: Primary Blue gradient or abstract visual
- Geometric pattern using brand wave motif
- Semi-abstract illustration or icon related to topic
- Adds visual interest without competing with title

**Accent Element**:
- Bright Cyan accent line (6px width) separating left/right sections
- OR subtle gradient overlay connecting both sections

---

### Alternative Layout: Image Background

**Full Background**:
- Related photo or abstract visual
- Dark overlay: rgba(10, 22, 40, 0.75) for text readability
- Gradient overlay (optional): Bottom-to-top gradient for better text contrast

**Content** (centered on overlay):
- Logo: White version, top-left, 60px padding, 48px size
- Article Title:
  - Inter Bold 700, 56px/64px, White
  - Center-aligned
  - Max width: 1000px
  - Text shadow: 0 2px 12px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3)
- Category Tag:
  - Pill shape: Primary Blue or Bright Cyan background
  - White text, Inter Semi-Bold 600, 18px
  - Position: Below title, centered
  - Padding: 12px 24px, border-radius: 24px

---

### Alternative Layout: Minimal Split

**Background**: White (#FFFFFF)

**Top Section** (1200x100px):
- Background: Light Gray (#F8FAFC)
- Logo + wordmark: Left-aligned, 60px padding
- "RunDailyAI Blog" text: Same line, after logo
- Border bottom: 3px solid Primary Blue

**Main Content** (centered in remaining space):
- Article Title:
  - Inter Extra Bold 800, 60px/68px, Deep Navy
  - Center-aligned
  - Max width: 1000px
  - Max 2 lines
- Subtitle or Excerpt (optional):
  - Inter Regular 400, 24px/32px, Slate Gray
  - Center-aligned below title
  - 32px spacing from title
  - Max 2 lines

**Bottom Section**:
- Decorative wave element: Primary Blue to Cyan gradient
- Height: 120px
- Abstract wave shape spanning full width
- Logo icon watermark (large, 15% opacity) in wave

---

### Design Elements

**Color Schemes for Blog Headers**:

1. **Professional Light**:
   - Background: White to Light Gray
   - Text: Deep Navy
   - Accents: Primary Blue

2. **Bold Dark**:
   - Background: Deep Navy
   - Text: White
   - Accents: Bright Cyan

3. **Gradient Premium**:
   - Background: Primary Blue to Bright Cyan gradient
   - White content card (900x480px) centered
   - Text: Deep Navy on white card

**Typography**:
- Headlines: 48-60px (must be readable at preview size)
- Subheadings: 20-28px
- Metadata: 16-18px
- Always use high contrast for readability

**Visual Elements**:
- Abstract geometric shapes (circles, rounded rectangles)
- Wave patterns from brand identity
- Subtle grid or dot patterns
- Icons (128-200px) related to article topic

---

### Export Settings

**Primary Export**:
- Format: JPEG (quality 90%) for smaller file size
- Dimensions: 1200x630px
- Color Space: RGB
- Resolution: 72 DPI
- File naming: `RDAI_BlogHeader_[ArticleSlug]_1200x630.jpg`

**Alternative**: PNG-24 if transparency or sharp text edges are critical

---

### Usage Guidelines

**Do**:
- Keep title under 80 characters for best readability
- Use high contrast (4.5:1 minimum) for text
- Test preview at 600px width (common preview size)
- Include logo for brand recognition
- Ensure title is readable without clicking
- Keep critical content in safe zone (avoid edges)

**Don't**:
- Use complex backgrounds that compete with text
- Make text too small (under 40px for headlines)
- Overcrowd with multiple messages
- Use low-contrast color combinations
- Forget to optimize file size (keep under 300KB)

---

### Platform-Specific Considerations

**Facebook**: Full 1200x630 visible, centers image
**LinkedIn**: Full 1200x630 visible, important for professional content
**Twitter/X**: Crops to 1200x628, very similar
**Slack**: Shows preview, crops edges slightly
**Discord**: Centers and scales, keep content centered

## Content Variations

### For Different Article Types

**How-To / Tutorial**:
- Use step indicators or numbered visual elements
- Bright, approachable color scheme
- Clear, action-oriented title

**Research / Data**:
- Include data visualization elements
- Professional color scheme (navy/white)
- Stats or key findings in design

**News / Updates**:
- Bold, attention-grabbing layout
- Date prominently featured
- "New" or "Update" badge element

**Opinion / Thought Leadership**:
- Author name may be included
- Sophisticated, minimal design
- Quote-style layout option
